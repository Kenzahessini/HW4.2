Homework 7: Interactive Map

Kenza Hessini

I chose to represent global carbon dioxide emissions for the year of 2020. I chose this topic because I thought it would be interesting to see which countries emitted the most CO2 in 2020. The darkest shades of purple represent the countries emiting the most CO2. We can see that the USA, China, India, and Russia emit the most CO2 while many African countries emit little CO2. There are also countries (represented in red) that lack data for the year 2020.
